Help and FAQs

GENERAL CLEANING SERVICES

What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We provide standard, deep, and specialized cleaning for residential and commercial spaces, including move-in/move-out, post-construction, and customized services.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?
Yes, our cleaners bring eco-friendly supplies and professional-grade equipment. However, we can use client-provided products upon request.
How do you handle fragile or valuable items?
We treat all items with care and avoid touching fragile or high-value objects without prior consent. Please notify us of any special requirements.
Are your cleaners background-checked and trained?
Yes, all our cleaners are background-checked, professionally trained, and insured to ensure a safe and high-quality experience.
How can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
You can easily reschedule or cancel by calling or emailing us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any rescheduling fees.

TRUST AND SAFETY

Can I trust my cleaning professional?
All of our folks are interviewed and pass a nationwide detailed background check. Only about 10% of the people that apply end up working as a Sub Cleaners certified cleaner.
What happens if something goes wrong during my appointment?
If something goes wrong, we’re here to make it right. Please let us know right away and we’ll be there to take care of it. That’s our promise to you!
Is my billing information kept safe and secure?
We have three levels of security in place. First off, our booking page is protected by extended validation SSL. Secondly, our booking form has its own layer of 256-bit security. Third, credit card transactions are processed by Stripe and are layered on their own 256 security protocol. In addition, no credit card numbers are stored in our system (only a token that allows us to charge the card). Rest assured, we take security very seriously.
Do the cleaning professionals go through a background check?
Yes, they do. We run a detailed nationwide background check on all applicants as the last step in our pre-screening process.
What is your 100% satisfaction guarantee policy?
If you’re not content with your cleaning, we will come out and re-clean (within a 7-day window). If you still don’t think we did a good enough job to recommend us to your friends, we’ll refund your money.
What is your refund policy?
We aim for complete satisfaction. If you’re not happy with any part of the service, contact us within 24 hours, and we’ll re-clean the area at no additional cost.

PRICING AND POLICIES

How are cleaning prices determined?
Our prices are based on the size of your home (square footage or number of bedrooms and bathrooms), the type of service, and any requested add-ons.
Are there any additional fees for special requests?
Yes, some special services like inside fridge, oven cleaning, or laundry may incur additional fees. Contact us for an exact quote based on your needs.
Do you offer recurring cleaning discounts?
Yes, we offer discounts for weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring services. Contact us for our latest packages and pricing options.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, online payments, and bank transfers. Payment is typically processed after the service is completed.
Is tipping required or expected?
Tips are appreciated but not required. If you’re satisfied with the service, a tip is a nice way to show appreciation to our cleaners.
What is your refund or satisfaction guarantee policy?
We aim for complete satisfaction. If you’re not happy with any part of the service, contact us within 24 hours, and we’ll re-clean the area at no additional cost.

COMMON CLEANING QUESTIONS

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
You do not need to be home during the cleaning. However, we ask that you provide access to the property and let us know if there are any special instructions or concerns.
What should I do before the cleaning team arrives?
To help us clean more efficiently, we recommend picking up any personal items from the floors and countertops. This ensures our cleaners can focus on the main tasks.
Can I request specific cleaning tasks?
Yes! You can add specific requests to your booking form or let the cleaner know directly. We’ll do our best to accommodate any special cleaning tasks.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?
If you’re not completely satisfied with the cleaning, contact us within 24 hours, and we’ll send someone to resolve the issue at no extra cost.
How long does the cleaning take?
The time it takes depends on the size of your space and the type of cleaning required. We can give you an estimated duration when you book your appointment.
Are there any items or surfaces you won't clean?
For safety reasons, we do not clean certain areas such as high windows and outdoor gutters. Please let us know if you have specific concerns.
